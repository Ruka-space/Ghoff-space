About Us

We aim to be one of the leading organizations in student exchange and to expand our projects in beach protection and environmental conservation. The foundation of Safrni is about building a family, not just organizing trips. We don't just offer vacations; we create memories.

The story began in 2017 when I was just a member in one of the organizations and realized that the focus wasn't on the journey or the client, but rather seeing it as a mere product. That's when I decided to make a change. In June 2020, I found myself with a group of people from all over the world, each with a different language and culture. It was then that I realized I was not just adding value to their lives but also learning from them. We were all changing each other's lives.

It was from this moment that I started to see myself as a representative of my country, my environment, and my home. I wanted to show the world Egypt from a different perspective. Over time, our team grew, and we started to develop further, creating more useful and impactful work. We expanded beyond Egypt and reached many other countries.

Safrni started with an idea, and that idea evolved into a company. The company grew into a family. Our mission continues to grow with us, creating more memories and meaningful connections.
